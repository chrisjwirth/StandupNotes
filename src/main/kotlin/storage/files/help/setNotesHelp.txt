Setting Standup Notes------------------------------------
1. To set your daily standup notes, enter either 's'
   or 'set' from the main menu (case-insensitive).
2. On the screen that appears, you will be able to enter
   your daily standup note in the configured format.
   - Guided (default): Provides prompts to aligned with
     traditional standup meetings.
   - Freeform: Provides a single unprompted note
     opportunity.
3. To change the note format, enter either 'c' or
   'config' from the main menu (case-insensitive) and
   follow the prompts therein.
