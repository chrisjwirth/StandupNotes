Getting Standup Notes------------------------------------
1. To get your daily standup notes, enter either 'g' or
   'get' from the main menu (case-insensitive).
2. Your daily standup notes will automatically be
   retrieved for the configured format.
   - Guided (default): Provides prompts to aligned with
     traditional standup meetings.
   - Freeform: Provides a single unprompted note
     opportunity.
